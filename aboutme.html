Question and Answer


When did I start to learn how to draw?

I started to draw when I was in elementary, I was also inspired by my classmate and close friend. I also love watching anime and
cartoons.

What are my inspirations?

I love all the movies that Makoto Shinkai made. Also the movies that was made by Ghibli Studios. A They really pay attention to
the details that they include. My works are also inspired by bright colors and modern style.

What are the medium I use?

I do both Traditional and Digital Art. In traditional art, I used colored pencils, markers and Fine line pens (sometimes also watercolors).
In digital art, I used art software like, paint tool sai, IbisPaintX, and ClipStudio Paint Pro. And Wacom intuous.
